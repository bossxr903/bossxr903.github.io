Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the buying process.

How do I schedule a private viewing?

To schedule a private viewing, you can contact our concierge team directly via phone, or fill out the inquiry form on the property's dedicated page. We require a brief verification process for our most exclusive listings to ensure privacy for our sellers.

Do you handle international transactions?

Absolutely. Over 40% of our clients are international buyers. We have a dedicated team of legal and financial experts who specialize in cross-border transactions, ensuring a smooth process regardless of your home country.

What is an off-market property?

Off-market properties (or pocket listings) are homes for sale that are not publicly advertised on multiple listing services (MLS). Because of our extensive network, we provide our clients access to these highly exclusive, private sales.
