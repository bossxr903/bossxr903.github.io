FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your visiting hours?
Our clinic is open for outpatient appointments from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Monday to Friday, and 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM on weekends. Our emergency room operates 24/7. In-patient visiting hours are typically from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM.
Do you accept health insurance?
Yes, we accept most major health insurance plans including Medicare, Blue Cross Blue Shield, UnitedHealthcare, Aetna, and Cigna. Please bring your insurance card during your visit, or call our billing department beforehand to confirm coverage details.
How can I book an appointment?
You can book an appointment easily by using the online appointment form on this website, or by calling our main reception desk at +1 (555) 123-4567. We also welcome walk-in patients for general check-ups depending on doctor availability.
What should I bring for my first visit?
Please bring a valid photo ID, your current health insurance card, a list of any medications you are currently taking, and any previous medical records or test results relevant to your visit.

Need Urgent Medical Help?

Our emergency department is open 24/7 to provide immediate care.
