About Our School

A Legacy of Excellence in Learning & Character

Founded in 1995, Horizon Academy has been at the forefront of quality education. We believe in holistic development, ensuring that our students excel not only in academics but also in sports, arts, and character building.

  • Modern curriculum aligned with global standards
  • State-of-the-art facilities and laboratories
  • Dedicated and highly qualified teaching staff
  • Focus on extracurricular and personal development
